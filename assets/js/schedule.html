History

When Anne Handley and Jean Meyer founded the Brooklyn Film Festival in 1939, their aim was to gather the countries most important people, showcasing the city and state as a major movie destination. Ofcourse, the very first edition wasn't as big as it is now.

The festival

For 79 years, the Festival has delighted audiences with outstanding interests in movies. Since the late 1970's the Brooklyn Film Festival became a non-profit, community-supported arts organization. Brooklyn bridge park

Brooklyn Bridge Park is a world-class waterfront park with rolling hills, riverfront promenades, lush gardens, and spectacular city views. Parking near Brooklyn Bridge Park is very limited. We highly recommend taking public transportation, biking or walking to the park. You can see the exact location of the park on the map.
